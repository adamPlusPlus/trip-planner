## Recommendations

### For Seeing Snow (Without Heavy Driving)
1. **Check app for current conditions**: Real-time forecasts available
2. **Drive carefully**: Check conditions, drive in daylight
3. **Have backup plan**: If conditions are too dangerous, use alternative route

### Driving Safety
- **Check road conditions**: State DOT websites
- **Drive in daylight**: Especially for mountain segments

- **4WD not required**: But be cautious in snow

- **Cold temperatures**: Definitely - dress appropriately
