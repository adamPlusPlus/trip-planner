## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Broken Bow, Oklahoma

#### Pros
- ✅ **Shortest drive**: Only 350 miles, easiest to reach
- ✅ **Most budget-friendly**: ~$578 for 4 nights (well under $1,000)
- ✅ **Least fuel cost**: Only $48 round trip
- ✅ **Dense pine forests**: Beavers Bend State Park, Ouachita NF
- ✅ **Very safe driving**: All major highways, no mountain passes
- ✅ **Scenic river**: Mountain Fork River through forests
- ✅ **Easy one-day drive**: Can arrive early afternoon

#### Cons
- ⚠️ **Lower snow potential**: Light snow possible but not guaranteed
- ⚠️ **Shorter distance**: Less "road trip" experience if that's desired

#### Best For
- Budget-conscious travelers
- Those wanting easiest/shortest drive
- Dense forest experience without long drive
- Piney Woods biome

#### Weather (Dec 28 - Jan 3)
- **Temps**: 21-69°F (cold mornings, mild afternoons)
- **Snow**: Low-moderate chance, light if any
- **Conditions**: Generally mild, safe driving

---

### 2. Hot Springs, Arkansas

#### Pros
- ✅ **Moderate drive**: 450 miles, manageable
- ✅ **Budget-friendly**: ~$640 for 4 nights (under $1,000)
- ✅ **Low fuel cost**: Only $60 round trip
- ✅ **Dense forests**: Ouachita National Forest (1.8M acres)
- ✅ **Very safe driving**: All major highways, no mountain passes
- ✅ **Unique feature**: Natural hot springs (great in winter!)
- ✅ **National Park**: Hot Springs NP (free entry!)

#### Cons
- ⚠️ **Lower snow potential**: Light snow possible but not guaranteed
- ⚠️ **Moderate distance**: Longer than Broken Bow

#### Best For
- Those wanting unique hot springs experience
- Moderate drive distance
- National park experience
- Dense forest + unique thermal features

#### Weather (Dec 28 - Jan 3)
- **Temps**: 23-67°F (cold mornings, mild afternoons)
- **Snow**: Low-moderate chance, light if any
- **Conditions**: Generally mild, safe driving

---

### 3. Branson, Missouri

#### Pros
- ✅ **Best snow potential**: Highest likelihood of seeing snow
- ✅ **Dense forests**: Mark Twain NF (1.5M acres), Ozark Mountains
- ✅ **Mountain scenery**: Ozark Mountains without dangerous driving
- ✅ **Very safe driving**: Major highways, no extreme mountain passes
- ✅ **Multiple attractions**: Table Rock Lake, Dogwood Canyon
- ✅ **Moderate budget**: ~$780 for 4 nights (under $1,000)

#### Cons
- ⚠️ **Longer drive**: 600 miles, 9 hours
- ⚠️ **Higher cost**: More expensive than other two options
- ⚠️ **Tourist area**: May have higher prices
- ⚠️ **Coldest**: Coldest temperatures of the three

#### Best For
- Those prioritizing snow viewing
- Mountain scenery without dangerous driving
- Longer road trip experience
- Best winter forest experience

#### Weather (Dec 28 - Jan 3)
- **Temps**: 19-65°F (coldest of the three)
- **Snow**: **Moderate-high chance** (best snow potential!)
- **Conditions**: Cold but manageable, safe driving
