# Things to See - Pagosa Springs, Colorado

## Focus: Nature, National Forests, Snow, Hot Springs

## Primary Attractions

### Wolf Creek Ski Area
- **Location**: ~20 miles from Pagosa Springs
- **Features**:
  - Major ski area with guaranteed snow
  - Mountain scenery
  - Dense forests
  - High elevation (10,300-11,904 ft)
- **Activities**:
  - Skiing/snowboarding (if interested)
  - Scenic viewing
  - Mountain hiking (summer)
- **Note**: Guaranteed snow, beautiful mountain setting

### [San Juan National Forest](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=San%20Juan%20National%20Forest)
- **Size**: 1.8 million acres
- **Features**:
  - Dense mountain forests
  - San Juan Mountains
  - Extensive trail system
  - Wildlife viewing
- **Activities**:
  - Hiking (hundreds of miles of trails)
  - Wildlife viewing
  - Scenic drives
  - Photography
- **Access**: Multiple access points near Pagosa Springs
- **Note**: Extensive forested areas, excellent for dense forest experience

### [Pagosa Hot Springs](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pagosa%20Hot%20Springs)
- **Location**: In Pagosa Springs
- **Features**:
  - World's deepest hot springs
  - Natural hot springs
  - Mountain setting
- **Activities**:
  - Hot springs soaking
  - Scenic viewing
- **Note**: Unique natural attraction, may have fees

### [San Juan River](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=San%20Juan%20River)
- **Location**: Through Pagosa Springs
- **Features**:
  - Scenic river
  - Forested banks
  - Mountain views
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
  - Fishing (summer)
- **Note**: Beautiful river setting

### [Chimney Rock National Monument](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Chimney%20Rock%20National%20Monument)
- **Location**: ~20 miles from Pagosa Springs
- **Features**:
  - Ancient ruins
  - Historic significance
  - Mountain views
- **Activities**:
  - Cultural tours
  - Photography
  - Learning about ancient culture
- **Note**: Unique cultural attraction, may have admission fee

## Secondary Attractions

### [Weminuche Wilderness](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Weminuche%20Wilderness)
- **Location**: In San Juan National Forest
- **Features**: Wilderness area, dense forests, remote
- **Note**: More remote, for experienced hikers

### [Piedra River](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Piedra%20River)
- **Location**: Near Pagosa Springs
- **Features**: Scenic river, forested setting
- **Note**: Additional scenic area

## Unique Biomes to Experience
1. **Mountain Forests**: Dense forests in San Juan National Forest
2. **Alpine Areas**: High elevation areas with guaranteed snow
3. **San Juan Mountains**: Classic Colorado mountain scenery
4. **Hot Springs**: World's deepest hot springs

## Hiking Recommendations

### [Easy to Moderate](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Easy%20to%20Moderate)
- **San Juan National Forest Trails**: Various lengths, well-maintained
- **San Juan River Trails**: Scenic river trails
- **Lower elevation forest trails**: Through dense forests

### [More Challenging](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=More%20Challenging)
- **Weminuche Wilderness**: Backcountry hiking
- **High elevation trails**: Advanced hiking
- **Longer San Juan NF Trails**: Extended hikes through forests

## Planning Considerations
- **Park Hours**: Check winter hours for facilities
- **Trail Conditions**: Many trails may be snowy/icy in winter
- **Weather**: Very cold, prepare for winter conditions
- **Snow**: Guaranteed snow viewing
- **Entrance Fees**: National forest is free, some attractions may have fees
- **Elevation**: High elevation - prepare for altitude and very cold

## Recommended Priority
1. **San Juan National Forest**: Extensive dense forest experience
2. **Wolf Creek Ski Area**: Guaranteed snow, mountain scenery
3. **Pagosa Hot Springs**: World's deepest hot springs
4. **San Juan River**: Scenic river through town
5. **Chimney Rock National Monument**: Unique cultural attraction

## Notes
- **Dense Forests**: San Juan National Forest provides extensive forested areas
- **Snow Viewing**: Guaranteed snow, especially at higher elevations
- **Nature Focus**: All attractions are nature-focused
- **Accessibility**: Generally good road access, trails vary in difficulty
- **Cold Weather**: Prepare for very cold conditions
- **Hot Springs**: Unique feature - world's deepest hot springs
