# Pagosa Springs, Colorado - Trip Overview

![Pagosa Springs Colorado](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1511497584788-876760111969?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Destination Summary
- **Distance from Houston**: ~900 miles (~13-14 hours driving)
- **Location**: Northwest of Houston, in the San Juan Mountains of Colorado
- **Primary Attraction**: Wolf Creek Ski Area + San Juan National Forest + Natural Hot Springs
- **Best For**: Guaranteed snow, natural hot springs, mountain forests, closer than Durango

### Weather Summary
- **Temperatures**: Very cold (5-14°F lows, 26-42°F highs)
- **Precipitation**: Heavy snow likely
- **Conditions**: Mountain conditions - very cold, variable
- **Elevation**: ~7,100 feet at Pagosa Springs

## Why This Destination?
✅ **Guaranteed Snow**: Very likely to see heavy snow
✅ **Natural Hot Springs**: World's deepest hot springs
✅ **Ski Area**: Wolf Creek Ski Area nearby, guaranteed snow
✅ **Mountain Forests**: San Juan National Forest - extensive forested areas
✅ **Closer Than Durango**: ~200 miles closer
✅ **Nature Focus**: National forest, ski area, hot springs, mountain scenery
⚠️ **Mountain Driving**: Mountain roads can be challenging

## Key Attractions
- **Wolf Creek Ski Area**: Major ski area, guaranteed snow
- **San Juan National Forest**: Extensive mountain forests, hiking
- **Pagosa Hot Springs**: World's deepest hot springs
- **San Juan River**: Scenic river through town
- **Chimney Rock National Monument**: Nearby ancient ruins

## Accommodation Notes
- Many properties in forest/mountain settings
- Kitchen facilities commonly available
- Options near San Juan National Forest
- Holiday period may have higher rates
- Mix of hot springs resorts and mountain cabins

## Driving Route
- **Primary Route**: I-10 W → I-35 N → I-40 W → US-64 W → US-84 W → US-160 W
- **Mountain Segment**: US-160 through mountains - check conditions
- **Stopover**: Amarillo or Santa Fe area recommended
- **Winter Hazards**: Mountain passes can be very challenging - check CDOT conditions

## Important Considerations
⚠️ **Mountain Driving**: Mountain passes can be very challenging
⚠️ **Mountain Weather**: Conditions can change quickly
⚠️ **Elevation**: High elevation - prepare for very cold temperatures
⚠️ **Road Closures**: Mountain passes can close during severe weather
