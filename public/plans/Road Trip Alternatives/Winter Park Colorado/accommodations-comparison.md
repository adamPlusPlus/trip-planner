# Winter Park, Colorado Accommodations Comparison

![Cabin in Forest](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1449824913935-59a10b8d2000?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Preferences
- **Location**: In forest or nature setting (preferred)
- **Amenities**: Kitchen facilities (preferred)
- **Budget**: Keep under $1000 total, can extend to $2000 if needed
- **Dates**: Dec 28 - Jan 1, 2, or 3 (depending on cost)
- **Duration**: 3-5 nights

## Research Notes
- Winter Park is a major ski resort destination - prices may be higher during holiday period
- Many properties near Arapaho National Forest
- Ski resort proximity may significantly increase prices
- Properties with kitchens can help save on food costs

## Comparison Criteria
- Price per night
- Kitchen availability
- Location (nature/forest vs. town)
- Reviews/ratings
- Cancellation policy
- Minimum stay requirements

## Cost-Saving Strategies
- **Kitchen availability**: Cook meals to save on dining costs
- **Book early**: Holiday period fills up quickly
- **Flexible dates**: Jan 1-3 may be slightly cheaper
- **Slightly outside town**: Properties in Fraser Valley may be cheaper

## Notes
- **Winter Park elevation**: ~9,000 feet
- **Weather**: Expect very cold, guaranteed snow
- **Some mountain properties may have challenging access in winter**
- **Verify property access roads are maintained in winter**
- **I-70 access**: Check road conditions before booking remote properties
