# Budget Planning - Winter Park, Colorado Road Trip

![Budget Planning](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1554224155-6726b3ff858f?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Budget Goals
- **Target**: Under $1,000 total
- **Maximum**: Up to $2,000 if needed
- **Dates**: Dec 28 - Jan 1, 2, or 3 (depending on cost)
- **Duration**: 3-5 nights in Winter Park + 2 nights stopover (total 5-7 nights)

## Budget Breakdown

### 1. Fuel Costs
- **Total Distance**: ~2,100 miles (round trip)
- **Vehicle**: Hybrid Honda Accord (~45-50 MPG highway)
- **Fuel Needed**: ~42-47 gallons
- **Cost per Gallon**: ~$3.00 (estimate, check current prices)
- **Estimated Fuel Cost**: **$126 - $141**
- **Note**: Similar to Durango distance

### 2. Accommodations

#### Winter Park (3-5 nights)
- **Target**: $120-200/night with kitchen
- **3 nights**: $360-600
- **4 nights**: $480-800
- **5 nights**: $600-1,000
- **Notes**:
  - Holiday period may have higher rates
  - Ski resort proximity may increase prices significantly
  - Properties with kitchens may cost more but save on food
  - Nature/forest settings may have premium pricing

#### Stopover Locations (2 nights total)
- **Going**: 1 night Amarillo/Palo Duro area
- **Coming**: 1 night Amarillo/Palo Duro area
- **Target**: $50-100/night
- **Estimated**: **$100-200**

**Total Accommodations**: **$460-1,200** (depending on Winter Park nights and rates)

### 3. Food & Groceries

#### Strategy: Cook at accommodations (kitchen preference)
- **Groceries for trip**: $150-250
- **Dining out**: $50-100 (occasional meals)
- **Total Food**: **$200-350**

### 4. Park Entrance Fees & Activities
- **Arapaho National Forest**: FREE
- **Rocky Mountain National Park**: $25-35 per vehicle (if visiting)
- **Ski area**: Optional, lift tickets if skiing
- **Estimated**: **$25-150**

### 5. Miscellaneous Expenses
- **Vehicle**: Tolls, parking, prep
- **Supplies**: Pre-trip shopping, emergency supplies
- **Other**: Souvenirs, unexpected
- **Total Miscellaneous**: **$125-320**

## Total Budget Estimate

### Scenario 1: Minimal Budget (Target $1,000)
- Fuel: $141
- Accommodations (3 nights + stopover): $500
- Food: $200
- Parks/Activities: $40
- Miscellaneous: $100
- **Total: ~$981** ✅ Just under budget!

### Scenario 2: Comfortable Budget ($1,500)
- Fuel: $141
- Accommodations (4 nights + stopover): $700
- Food: $250
- Parks/Activities: $80
- Miscellaneous: $200
- **Total: ~$1,371** ✅ Under budget!

### Scenario 3: Extended Stay ($2,000)
- Fuel: $141
- Accommodations (5 nights + stopover): $900
- Food: $300
- Parks/Activities: $150
- Miscellaneous: $300
- **Total: ~$1,791** ✅ Under budget!

## Cost-Saving Strategies
- **Kitchen availability**: Cook meals to save on dining costs
- **Book early**: Holiday period fills up quickly, early booking gets better rates
- **Flexible dates**: Jan 1-3 may be slightly cheaper than Dec 28-31
- **Slightly outside town**: Properties further from ski area may be cheaper

## Notes
- **Ski resort pricing**: May have premium rates during holiday period
- **Kitchen is critical**: Saves $200-400 on food costs
- **Similar distance to Durango**: Fuel costs similar
