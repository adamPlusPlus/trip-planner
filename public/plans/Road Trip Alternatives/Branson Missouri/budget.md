## Cost-Saving Strategies

### Accommodations
1. **Book early**: Holiday period fills up, early booking gets better rates
2. **Kitchen availability**: Cook meals instead of dining out (saves $200-400)
3. **Flexible dates**: Jan 1-3 may be slightly cheaper than Dec 28-31
4. **Slightly outside Branson**: Properties further from town may be cheaper
5. **Longer stays**: Some properties offer discounts for 4+ nights
6. **State park cabins**: Often more affordable than private rentals

### Food
1. **Cook at accommodations**: Major savings vs. dining out
2. **Bring non-perishables**: Snacks, some meals from home
3. **Shop at grocery stores**: Not convenience stores
4. **Pack lunches**: For day trips and driving

### Activities
1. **Free activities**: Mark Twain NF is free! Hiking, scenic drives
2. **State park fees**: Minimal ($3-7 per park)
3. **Dogwood Canyon**: Optional, can skip if budget tight
4. **Self-guided**: Avoid expensive guided tours when possible

### Fuel
1. **Hybrid efficiency**: Already helps significantly
2. **Moderate distance**: 1,200 miles total (less than Durango)
3. **Drive efficiently**: Smooth acceleration, maintain speed
