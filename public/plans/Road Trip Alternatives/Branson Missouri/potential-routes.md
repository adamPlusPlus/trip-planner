## Route Overview
- **Total Distance**: ~600 miles
- **Total Drive Time**: ~9 hours (without stops)
- **Strategy**: Break up into 2 days, or long one-day drive

## Primary Route (Recommended)

### Houston → Branson via I-45 N and US-69 N

#### Detailed Route
1. **Houston to Dallas**: ~240 miles, ~3.5 hours
   - I-45 N
   - Major interstate, well-maintained
   - Good stopover option

2. **Dallas to Tulsa**: ~260 miles, ~4 hours
   - I-45 N to US-75 N (through McKinney)
   - Or: I-35 E to I-44 W (alternative)
   - Cross into Oklahoma
   - Well-maintained highways

3. **Tulsa to Branson**: ~200 miles, ~3 hours
   - US-412 W to US-65 N
   - Enter Missouri
   - Approach Ozark Mountains
   - Scenic approach to Branson

#### Advantages
- Most direct route
- Major highways (interstates and US highways)
- Well-maintained roads
- Minimal winter hazards (check Missouri segment)
- Good fuel efficiency
- Multiple stopover options (Dallas, Tulsa)

## Recommended Strategy

### Option 1: Two-Day Drive (Recommended)
- **Day 1**: Houston to Tulsa (~7 hours)
  - Stopover in Tulsa area
  - Break up the drive
- **Day 2**: Tulsa to Branson (~3 hours)
  - Shorter drive day
  - Arrive Branson, check in

### Option 2: One-Day Drive
- **Day 1**: Houston to Branson (~9 hours)
  - Leave very early morning
  - Arrive late afternoon/evening
  - Long day but doable

## Stopover Options

### Dallas Area
- **Distance from Houston**: ~240 miles
- **Options**: Various hotels, Airbnbs
- **Note**: Urban area, not nature-focused, but convenient

### Tulsa Area
- **Distance from Houston**: ~500 miles
- **Options**: Hotels, some nature areas nearby
- **Note**: Good halfway point, breaks up drive nicely

## Weather Considerations

### Texas (Houston to Dallas)
- **December/January**: Mild to cold
- **Snow**: Rare
- **Roads**: Generally clear, well-maintained
- **Concern**: Low

### Oklahoma (Dallas to Tulsa)
- **December/January**: Cold, possible light snow
- **Roads**: Generally well-maintained
- **Concern**: Low to moderate

### Missouri (Tulsa to Branson)
- **December/January**: Cold, possible snow
- **Elevation**: Increases in Ozark Mountains
- **Roads**: Generally well-maintained, but check conditions
- **Concern**: Moderate (check Missouri DOT for conditions)
