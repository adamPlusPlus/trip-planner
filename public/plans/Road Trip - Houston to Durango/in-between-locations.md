# In-Between Locations to Stay Overnight - Durango, Colorado


## Requirements
- **One stop going** (Houston → Durango)
- **One stop coming** (Durango → Houston)
- **Purpose**: Break up the ~15-16 hour drive
- **Preferences**: Nature/forest settings preferred, kitchen preferred
- **Budget**: Keep costs reasonable (part of overall $1000-2000 budget)

## Drive Overview
- **Total Distance**: ~1,100 miles (Houston to Durango)
- **Drive Time**: ~15-16 hours (without stops)
- **Strategy**: Break into 2 days each direction (~7-8 hours driving per day)

## Going: Houston → Durango

### Option 1: Northern Route (Recommended for Nature)
**Houston → Amarillo Area → Durango**

### [Amarillo, TX Area](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Amarillo%2C%20TX%20Area)
- **Distance from Houston**: ~600 miles (~9 hours)
- **Distance to Durango**: ~500 miles (~7 hours)
- **Nature Options**: Palo Duro Canyon State Park, Caprock Canyons State Park
- **Lodging Research Needed**:
  - [ ] Research: Hotels with kitchenettes
  - [ ] Research: Airbnbs near Palo Duro Canyon
  - [ ] Check: Properties in/near state parks

### [Palo Duro Canyon State Park (30 min from Amarillo)](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Palo%20Duro%20Canyon%20State%20Park)
- **Location**: 30 minutes from Amarillo
- **Features**:
  - Camping available (check winter availability)
  - Cabins may be available
  - In-park accommodations
- **Note**: Major nature attraction, perfect stopover location

### [Caprock Canyons State Park (1.5 hours from Amarillo)](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Caprock%20Canyons%20State%20Park)
- **Location**: 1.5 hours from Amarillo
- **Features**:
  - More remote
  - Camping/cabin options
- **Note**: Alternative to Palo Duro if time allows

### [Lubbock, TX Area](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lubbock%2C%20TX%20Area)
- **Distance from Houston**: ~530 miles (~8 hours)
- **Distance to Durango**: ~570 miles (~8 hours)
- **Nature Options**: Fewer immediate nature options
- **Note**: More urban, less ideal for preferences

### Option 2: Southern Route (More Desert, Longer)
**Houston → El Paso/Albuquerque Area → Durango**

- **Features**:

### [Jemez Springs area](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jemez%20Springs%20area)

### [Las Cruces, NM Area](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Las%20Cruces%2C%20NM%20Area)

## Recommended Strategy

- **Day 1**: Houston to Amarillo/Palo Duro Canyon area (~9 hours)
  - Stay: Palo Duro Canyon State Park or nearby nature property
- **Day 2**: Amarillo area to Durango (~7 hours)
  - Arrive Durango, check in

- **Day 1**: Durango to Amarillo/Palo Duro Canyon area (~7 hours)
