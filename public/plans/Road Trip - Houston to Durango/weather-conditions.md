## Durango, Colorado Weather

### Temperature Expectations
- **Average High**: 32-54°F (0-12°C)
- **Average Low**: 21-31°F (-6 to -1°C)
- **Note**: Temperatures can vary significantly day to day

### Snow Conditions
- **December Average**: ~14.5 inches of snow over ~12 days
- **January**: Similar snow patterns
- **Expectation**: Moderate snow likely during your stay
- **Snow on ground**: Likely to see snow-covered landscapes
- **Driving in snow**: Possible but conditions vary

## Route Weather Considerations

### Texas (Houston to Amarillo)
- **December/January**: Mild to cold
- **Snow**: Rare in Houston/Austin, possible in Panhandle
- **Roads**: Generally clear, well-maintained
- **Concern**: Low

### New Mexico (Amarillo to Colorado border)
- **December/January**: Cold, possible snow
- **Elevation**: Increases gradually
- **Roads**: I-40 generally well-maintained
- **Concern**: Moderate (watch for winter weather)

### Colorado (Border to Durango) - CRITICAL SEGMENT
- **US-550 (Million Dollar Highway)**: 
  - Can be challenging in winter
  - May have snow/ice, especially at higher elevations
  - **Check CDOT conditions before driving this segment**
- **Elevation**: 6,500+ feet at Durango
- **Concern**: High - check conditions daily

## Recommendations

### For Seeing Snow (Without Heavy Driving)
1. **Stay in Durango area**: Snow on ground, scenic views
2. **Drive carefully on US-550**: Check conditions, drive in daylight
3. **Have backup plan**: If US-550 is too dangerous, use alternative route
4. **Monitor weather**: Check forecasts and road conditions daily

### Driving Safety
- **Check CDOT**: Colorado Department of Transportation road conditions
- **Drive in daylight**: Especially for mountain segments
- **Have chains**: May be required (check compatibility with hybrid Accord)
- **Flexible timing**: Be prepared to delay if conditions are dangerous
- **4WD not required**: But be cautious in snow

### What to Expect
- **Snow on ground**: Very likely in Durango area
- **Snow while driving**: Possible, especially on US-550
- **Heavy snow**: Less likely but possible - monitor forecasts
- **Cold temperatures**: Definitely - dress appropriately
