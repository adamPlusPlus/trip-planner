## Cost-Saving Strategies

### Accommodations
1. **Book early**: Holiday period fills up, early booking gets better rates
2. **Kitchen availability**: Cook meals instead of dining out (saves $300-500)
3. **Flexible dates**: Jan 1-3 may be slightly cheaper than Dec 28-31
4. **Slightly outside town**: Properties further from Durango may be cheaper
5. **Longer stays**: Some properties offer discounts for 4+ nights
6. **State park cabins**: Often more affordable than hotels/Airbnbs

### Food
1. **Cook at accommodations**: Major savings vs. dining out
2. **Bring non-perishables**: Snacks, some meals from home
3. **Shop at grocery stores**: Not convenience stores
4. **Pack lunches**: For day trips and driving

### Activities
1. **Free activities**: Hiking, scenic drives, nature viewing
2. **Park passes**: If visiting multiple parks, consider annual pass (if makes sense)
3. **Self-guided**: Avoid expensive guided tours when possible

### Fuel
1. **Hybrid efficiency**: Already helps significantly
2. **Drive efficiently**: Smooth acceleration, maintain speed
3. **Plan route**: Shortest route (Route 1) saves fuel
