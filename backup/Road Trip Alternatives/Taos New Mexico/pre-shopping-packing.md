# Pre-Shopping & Packing List - Taos, New Mexico


## Clothing & Personal Items
- [ ] Warm base layers (thermal underwear)
- [ ] Insulated mid-layers (fleece, wool sweaters)
- [ ] Waterproof/windproof outer jacket
- [ ] Warm gloves
- [ ] Warm hat/beanie
- [ ] Scarf or neck gaiter
- [ ] Warm socks (wool, multiple pairs)
- [ ] Insulated waterproof boots (for hiking)
- [ ] Regular comfortable shoes for driving
- [ ] Sunglasses
- [ ] Personal hygiene items
- [ ] Prescription medications

## Vehicle Essentials
- [ ] Ice scraper and snow brush
- [ ] Emergency kit (first aid, flashlight, batteries)
- [ ] Jumper cables or portable battery starter
- [ ] Tire pressure gauge
- [ ] Spare tire check
- [ ] Emergency blanket
- [ ] Portable phone charger/power bank
- [ ] GPS device or ensure phone GPS works offline
- [ ] Chains or traction devices (check compatibility with hybrid Accord)

## Food & Cooking Supplies
- [ ] Non-perishable snacks (trail mix, granola bars, nuts)
- [ ] Bottled water (multiple gallons)
- [ ] Portable cooler (if bringing perishables)
- [ ] Basic cooking utensils (if staying in places with kitchens)
- [ ] Reusable water bottles
- [ ] Coffee/tea supplies (if desired)
- [ ] Easy-to-prepare meals (if cooking at accommodations)

## Gear & Equipment
- [ ] Binoculars (for wildlife viewing)
- [ ] Camera or phone with good camera
- [ ] Backpack for day hikes
- [ ] Trekking poles (optional, for hiking)
- [ ] Maps (physical backup, even if using GPS)
- [ ] National/State Park passes (if you have annual pass)

## Safety & Navigation
- [ ] First aid kit (comprehensive)
- [ ] Emergency contact information
- [ ] Insurance cards (auto, health)
- [ ] Vehicle registration
- [ ] Driver's license
- [ ] Roadside assistance membership info
- [ ] Weather radio or app access
- [ ] NM DOT road conditions app/bookmark

## Miscellaneous
- [ ] Sunscreen (even in winter, high elevation)
- [ ] Lip balm (with SPF)
- [ ] Hand warmers (disposable)
- [ ] Cash (some areas may not accept cards)
- [ ] Credit/debit cards
- [ ] Reservation confirmations (printed or digital)
- [ ] Travel documents

## Pre-Trip Vehicle Check
- [ ] Oil change (if due)
- [ ] Tire tread check
- [ ] Battery check
- [ ] Brake inspection
- [ ] Windshield wipers (good condition)
- [ ] Windshield washer fluid
- [ ] Antifreeze/coolant check
- [ ] Lights check (headlights, taillights, turn signals)
- [ ] Hybrid system check (if service due)

## Notes
- **Distance**: ~900 miles - closer than Durango
- **Weather**: Very cold, guaranteed snow, mountain conditions
- **Mountain driving**: US-64 can be challenging in winter - check NM DOT road conditions before driving mountain segments
- **Elevation**: ~6,900 feet - prepare for altitude and cold, sunscreen important at elevation
- **Kitchen accommodations**: Bring basic spices, oils if you plan to cook
- **Snow chains**: May be required on mountain passes - check conditions and requirements
