# Things to See - Taos, New Mexico

## Focus: Nature, National Forests, Snow, Mountains

## Primary Attractions

### [Taos Ski Valley](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Taos%20Ski%20Valley)
- **Location**: ~20 miles from Taos
- **Features**:
  - Ski area with guaranteed snow
  - Mountain scenery
  - Dense forests
  - High elevation (9,200-12,481 ft)
- **Activities**:
  - Skiing/snowboarding (if interested)
  - Scenic viewing
  - Mountain hiking (summer)
- **Note**: Guaranteed snow, beautiful mountain setting

### [Carson National Forest](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Carson%20National%20Forest)
- **Size**: 1.5 million acres
- **Features**:
  - Dense mountain forests
  - Sangre de Cristo Mountains
  - Extensive trail system
  - Wildlife viewing
- **Activities**:
  - Hiking (hundreds of miles of trails)
  - Wildlife viewing
  - Scenic drives
  - Photography
- **Access**: Multiple access points near Taos
- **Note**: Extensive forested areas, excellent for dense forest experience

### [Taos Pueblo](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Taos%20Pueblo)
- **Location**: Near Taos
- **Features**:
  - UNESCO World Heritage Site
  - Ancient adobe structures
  - Unique culture
  - Historic significance
- **Activities**:
  - Cultural tours
  - Photography
  - Learning about Native American culture
- **Note**: Unique cultural attraction, may have admission fee

### [Rio Grande Gorge](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rio%20Grande%20Gorge)
- **Location**: Near Taos
- **Features**:
  - Deep canyon (800 ft deep)
  - Rio Grande River
  - Scenic views
  - Bridge overlook
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
  - Hiking nearby
- **Note**: Impressive canyon, scenic beauty

### [Wheeler Peak](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Wheeler%20Peak)
- **Location**: In Carson National Forest
- **Features**:
  - Highest point in New Mexico (13,161 ft)
  - Mountain views
  - Dense forests at lower elevations
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
  - Advanced hiking (summer)
- **Note**: Highest peak, impressive views

## Secondary Attractions

### [Enchanted Circle Scenic Byway](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Enchanted%20Circle%20Scenic%20Byway)
- **Length**: 84 miles
- **Features**: Scenic drive through mountains and forests
- **Note**: Beautiful mountain drive, check road conditions in winter

### Wild Rivers Recreation Area
- **Location**: Near Taos
- **Features**: Rio Grande Gorge area, hiking, scenic views
- **Note**: Additional nature area

### Orilla Verde Recreation Area
- **Location**: Near Taos
- **Features**: Rio Grande area, camping, hiking
- **Note**: River recreation area

## Unique Biomes to Experience
1. **Mountain Forests**: Dense forests in Carson National Forest
2. **High Desert Mountains**: Unique elevation-based ecosystems
3. **Alpine Areas**: High elevation areas with snow
4. **River Gorge**: Rio Grande Gorge ecosystem

## Hiking Recommendations

### [Easy to Moderate](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Easy%20to%20Moderate)
- **Carson National Forest Trails**: Various lengths, well-maintained
- **Rio Grande Gorge Trails**: Scenic river/canyon trails
- **Lower elevation forest trails**: Through dense forests

### [More Challenging](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=More%20Challenging)
- **Wheeler Peak area**: High elevation, advanced hiking
- **Longer Carson NF Trails**: Extended hikes through forests

## Planning Considerations
- **Park Hours**: Check winter hours for facilities
- **Trail Conditions**: Many trails may be snowy/icy in winter
- **Weather**: Very cold, prepare for winter conditions
- **Snow**: Guaranteed snow viewing
- **Entrance Fees**: National forest is free, some attractions may have fees
- **Elevation**: High elevation - prepare for altitude

## Recommended Priority
1. **Carson National Forest**: Extensive dense forest experience
2. **Taos Ski Valley**: Guaranteed snow, mountain scenery
3. **Rio Grande Gorge**: Impressive canyon, scenic views
4. **Taos Pueblo**: Unique cultural attraction
5. **Scenic drives**: Explore mountain areas

## Notes
- **Dense Forests**: Carson National Forest provides extensive forested areas
- **Snow Viewing**: Guaranteed snow, especially at higher elevations
- **Nature Focus**: All attractions are nature-focused
- **Accessibility**: Generally good road access, trails vary in difficulty
- **Cold Weather**: Prepare for very cold conditions
