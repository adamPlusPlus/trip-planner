# Potential Routes - Houston to Winter Park, Colorado

[🗺️ View Route on Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=1912+West+Dallas+Street%2C+Houston%2C+TX+77019&destination=Winter+Park%2C+CO&waypoints=Amarillo%2C+TX)

## Route Overview
- **Total Distance**: ~1,050 miles
- **Total Drive Time**: ~15-16 hours (without stops)
- **Strategy**: Break up into 2 days each direction

## Primary Route (Recommended)

### Houston → Winter Park via I-10 W, I-40 W, I-25 N, I-70 W, US-40 W

#### Detailed Route
1. **Houston to Amarillo**: ~600 miles, ~9 hours
   - I-10 W → I-35 N → US-87 N → I-40 W
   - Stopover location (Palo Duro Canyon)

2. **Amarillo to Denver**: ~450 miles, ~6.5 hours
   - I-40 W → I-25 N
   - Enter Colorado
   - Elevation increases

3. **Denver to Winter Park**: ~70 miles, ~1.5 hours
   - I-70 W → US-40 W
   - Mountain driving begins
   - **Note**: I-70 and US-40 through mountains can be very challenging in winter

#### Advantages
- Most direct route
- Good stopover point (Amarillo/Palo Duro Canyon or Denver area)
- Generally good road conditions (interstates until final segment)

## Recommended Strategy

### Going: Houston → Amarillo → Winter Park
- **Day 1**: Houston to Amarillo/Palo Duro Canyon (~9 hours)
  - Stop: Palo Duro Canyon State Park
  - Stay: Park cabin or nearby nature property
- **Day 2**: Amarillo to Winter Park (~8 hours)
  - Check I-70 and US-40 conditions before departure
  - Drive carefully in mountain segment
  - Arrive Winter Park, check in
