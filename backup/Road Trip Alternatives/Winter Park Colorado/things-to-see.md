# Things to See - Winter Park, Colorado

## Focus: Nature, National Forests, Snow, Mountains

## Primary Attractions

### [Winter Park Resort](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Winter%20Park%20Resort)
- **Location**: Main attraction in Winter Park
- **Features**:
  - Major ski area with guaranteed snow
  - Mountain scenery
  - Dense forests
  - High elevation (9,000-12,060 ft)
- **Activities**:
  - Skiing/snowboarding (if interested)
  - Scenic viewing
  - Mountain hiking (summer)
- **Note**: Guaranteed snow, beautiful mountain setting

### [Arapaho National Forest](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Arapaho%20National%20Forest)
- **Size**: 1.5 million acres
- **Features**:
  - Dense mountain forests
  - Rocky Mountains
  - Extensive trail system
  - Wildlife viewing
- **Activities**:
  - Hiking (hundreds of miles of trails)
  - Wildlife viewing
  - Scenic drives
  - Photography
- **Access**: Multiple access points near Winter Park
- **Note**: Extensive forested areas, excellent for dense forest experience

### [Rocky Mountain National Park](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rocky%20Mountain%20National%20Park)
- **Location**: ~30 miles from Winter Park
- **Features**:
  - World-class national park
  - Mountain scenery
  - Dense forests
  - Wildlife viewing
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
  - Hiking (summer)
- **Note**: May have entrance fees, check winter access

### [Fraser Valley](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fraser%20Valley)
- **Location**: Near Winter Park
- **Features**:
  - Scenic mountain valley
  - Forested areas
  - Mountain views
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
- **Note**: Beautiful mountain setting

### [Continental Divide](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Continental%20Divide)
- **Location**: Nearby
- **Features**:
  - Geographic landmark
  - Scenic views
  - Mountain scenery
- **Activities**:
  - Scenic viewing
  - Photography
- **Note**: Unique geographic feature

## Secondary Attractions

### [Berthoud Pass](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Berthoud%20Pass)
- **Location**: Near Winter Park
- **Features**: Mountain pass, scenic views
- **Note**: Check road conditions in winter

### Mary Jane Ski Area
- **Location**: Part of Winter Park Resort
- **Features**: Additional ski terrain
- **Note**: Guaranteed snow

## Unique Biomes to Experience
1. **Mountain Forests**: Dense forests in Arapaho National Forest
2. **Alpine Areas**: High elevation areas with guaranteed snow
3. **Rocky Mountains**: Classic Colorado mountain scenery

## Hiking Recommendations

### [Easy to Moderate](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Easy%20to%20Moderate)
- **Arapaho National Forest Trails**: Various lengths, well-maintained
- **Lower elevation forest trails**: Through dense forests

### [More Challenging](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=More%20Challenging)
- **High elevation trails**: Advanced hiking
- **Longer Arapaho NF Trails**: Extended hikes through forests

## Planning Considerations
- **Park Hours**: Check winter hours for facilities
- **Trail Conditions**: Many trails may be snowy/icy in winter
- **Weather**: Very cold, prepare for winter conditions
- **Snow**: Guaranteed snow viewing
- **Entrance Fees**: National forest is free, Rocky Mountain NP has fees
- **Elevation**: High elevation - prepare for altitude and very cold

## Recommended Priority
1. **Arapaho National Forest**: Extensive dense forest experience
2. **Winter Park Resort**: Guaranteed snow, mountain scenery
3. **Rocky Mountain National Park**: World-class park nearby
4. **Fraser Valley**: Scenic mountain setting
5. **Scenic drives**: Explore mountain areas

## Notes
- **Dense Forests**: Arapaho National Forest provides extensive forested areas
- **Snow Viewing**: Guaranteed snow, especially at higher elevations
- **Nature Focus**: All attractions are nature-focused
- **Accessibility**: Generally good road access, trails vary in difficulty
- **Cold Weather**: Prepare for very cold conditions
