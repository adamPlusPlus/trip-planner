# Branson, Missouri - Trip Overview

![Branson Missouri](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1511497584788-876760111969?w=800&h=600&fit=crop)

## Destination Summary
- **Distance from Houston**: ~600 miles (~9 hours driving)
- **Location**: North of Houston, in the Ozark Mountains
- **Primary Attraction**: Ozark Mountains + Mark Twain National Forest + Table Rock Lake
- **Best For**: Dense forests, higher snow likelihood, mountain scenery without extreme driving

### Snow Expectations
- **Snow Likelihood**: Moderate to high (best of the three options)

- **Precipitation**: Some rain, good chance of snow

## Why This Destination?
✅ **Dense Forests**: Ozark Mountains and Mark Twain National Forest
✅ **Best Snow Potential**: Highest likelihood of seeing snow among the three options
✅ **Safe Driving**: Major highways, no extreme mountain passes like US-550
✅ **Nature Focus**: Multiple state parks, national forest, Table Rock Lake
✅ **Mountain Scenery**: Ozark Mountains without dangerous driving
✅ **Unique Biomes**: Ozark highlands, forested mountains, lake ecosystems

## Key Attractions
- **Mark Twain National Forest**: Extensive hiking trails through dense woodlands
- **Table Rock State Park**: Scenic lake, hiking trails, dense forests
- **Dogwood Canyon Nature Park**: Waterfalls, trails, diverse wildlife
- **Ozark Scenic Byways**: Forested mountain drives
- **Hercules Glades Wilderness**: Remote forested area for hiking

## Accommodation Notes
- Many cabins in forested settings, especially near Table Rock Lake
- Kitchen facilities commonly available
- Options in secluded forest locations
- Holiday pricing may be higher (tourist area)

## Driving Route
- **Primary Route**: I-45 N → US-69 N (through Dallas, Tulsa area)
- **Road Conditions**: Major highways, generally well-maintained
- **Winter Hazards**: Low to moderate - check for snow/ice, especially in Missouri
- **Stopover Options**: Dallas area, Tulsa area, or Springfield MO area
