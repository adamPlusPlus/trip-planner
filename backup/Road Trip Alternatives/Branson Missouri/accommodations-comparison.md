## Preferences
- **Location**: In forest or nature setting (preferred)
- **Amenities**: Kitchen facilities (preferred)
- **Budget**: Keep under $1000 total, can extend to $2000 if needed
- **Dates**: Dec 28 - Jan 1, 2, or 3 (depending on cost)
- **Duration**: 3-5 nights

## Research Notes
- Branson is a major tourist destination - prices may be higher during holiday period
- Many properties near Table Rock Lake or in forested areas
- Book early for best rates and availability
- Properties with kitchens can help save on food costs

## Comparison Criteria
- Price per night
- Kitchen availability
- Location (nature/forest vs. town)
- Reviews/ratings
- Cancellation policy
- Minimum stay requirements

## Cost-Saving Strategies
- **Kitchen availability**: Cook meals to save on dining costs
- **Book early**: Holiday period fills up quickly
- **Flexible dates**: Jan 1-3 may be slightly cheaper than Dec 28-31
- **Slightly outside Branson**: Properties further from town may be cheaper
- **Longer stays**: Some properties offer discounts for 4+ nights
- **State park cabins**: Often more affordable than private rentals
