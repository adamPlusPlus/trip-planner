# In-Between Locations to Stay Overnight - Hot Springs, Arkansas

## Requirements
- **Optional stopover**: Drive is manageable (7 hours) in one day, but can break up if desired
- **If desired**: One stop going and/or coming
- **Preferences**: Nature/forest settings preferred, kitchen preferred
- **Budget**: Keep costs reasonable (part of overall budget)

## Drive Overview
- **Total Distance**: ~450 miles (Houston to Hot Springs)
- **Drive Time**: ~7 hours (without stops)
- **Strategy**: Can do in one day, or break up with stopover

## Going: Houston → Hot Springs

### Option 1: One-Day Drive
**Houston → Hot Springs**
- **Distance**: ~450 miles
- **Time**: ~7 hours
- **Note**: Manageable in one day, leave early morning

**Houston → Dallas Area → Hot Springs**
